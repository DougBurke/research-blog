About

This is a "live blog" of my analysis of the possible group of galaxies found in a Chandra observation of the galaxy cluster MACS J0717.5+3745. It is intended both to provide useful science (analysis of the object) but also to explore blogging/Open Access Science. More information can be found on the Introduction page.

The github account containing the source for this blog is also available.

I am Doug Burke, as Astrophysicist at the Chandra X-ray Center.
